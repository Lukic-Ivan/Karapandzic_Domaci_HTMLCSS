Our Team

Our team at Vintage Disco brings the energy to every night. Our skilled bartenders craft vintage-inspired cocktails, while our attentive waitstaff make sure you have everything you need to keep the good vibes going. And our talented DJs spin classic disco tracks, lighting up the dance floor with ‘70s and ‘80s grooves. Together, they create the perfect mix of music, service, and style for an unforgettable experience. Do you want to join us? Contact us here!

Our Mission

Our mission is to revive the disco era’s unique charm by hosting events, parties, and gatherings where people can dance, connect, and enjoy timeless music. We believe that the joy of disco lies in its inclusivity.
